Rebranding Radha Nagar

Rebranding Radha Nagar with a fruit processing factory

Introduction

We had the privilege of going to a field trip in Sylhet on March 2018 thanks to our honorable faculty AIY. It was an enlightening experience for us. The goal was to survey the surrounding area of Radha Nagar and, choose a place for rebranding. The place we choose and our choice had to be viable for Radha Nagar. It had to be something that would be long term and sustainable for Radha Nagar. Our choice was a fruit processing factory. That would bring employment and refreshing changes to the place.
